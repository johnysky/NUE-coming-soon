About Naturally Urban Environmental Inc.

Naturally Urban Environmental Incorporated is a sustainable construction, environmental, renewable energy firm. We a for-profit corporation that was established to contribute to the improvement of the environmental integrity of our communities and municipalities through sustainable construction practises, renewable energy integration, workforce development and community education towards sustaimable practices and lifestyle. N.U.E. Inc, is focused on the collaboration of social service and education in an effort to provide sustainable, eco-friendly and green products, services and workmanship to meet the demand for reducing energy consumption, raw materials and toxic products that are endangering the qualitiy of lives in our communities.
